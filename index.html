ABOUT US

Singum studios generally consist of three rooms: the studio itself, where the sound for the recording is created, the control room, where the sound from the studio is recorded and manipulated, and the machine room, where noisier equipment that may interfere with the recording process is kept.

 

  • Providing recording and rehearsal space to musicians throughout the area.
  • Mixing is the process of combining the different sonic elements of recorded music.
  • Providing top audio mastering services for local and national artists and production companies.
